Manage Advance payment of Sales order

Key Features:

  • Allow user to manage the Customers Advance payment for the Sales order.
  • Manage with Multi Company & Multi Currency.

Overview

  • From Sales order, user have option to create Advance payment of that particular sales order.
  • In opened pop-up, select the Payment journal, Payment status, Payment Amount and click on button Create Advance Payment.
  • In Sales order, there is the button for view the advance payment.
  • List of Advance Payment of that particular sales order.
  • After create invoice of sales order, user can see the message for outstanding payments of customer and able to use that Advance payment for paying the invoice.
